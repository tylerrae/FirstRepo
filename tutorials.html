Makeup Basics

Say you're really interested in makeup. You may not feel that you're not good at it and you start comparing to others. It's important to remember that it takes time to get better at a skill that you like. It's always imporant to remember that even if you're really good at that specific skill. There's always room to improve no mattter what you're good at.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Less is more. This is a vital part of makeup unless of course it's purposely done for the look.It's also better to start with a natural look for beginners.
  • It's always good to determine yur face shape and your eye shape so that your makeup can flatter your face and eyes.
  • Use colors that make your eye color pop!
