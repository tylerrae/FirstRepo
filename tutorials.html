Makeup Basics

Say you're really interested in makeup. You may not feel that you're not good at it and you start comparing yourself to others. It's important to remember that it takes time to get better at a skill that you like. It's always imporant to remember that, even if you're really good at that specific skill. There's always room to improve no mattter what you're good at.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Less is more.

    • This is a vital part of makeup unless of course it's purposely done for the look. It's also better to start with a natural look for beginners.

  • It's always good to determine your eye shape.

    • So that your makeup looks flawless when it flatters your eyes.

  • Use colors that make your eye color pop!
    • and if you are using colors, use colors that will bring attention to your eyes.

  • Make sure to match your complexion with your makeup.

    • Make sure they compliment each other so that your makeup can look nice.

When starting with makeup, it's good to start small and work your way up. You don't need to do the most complicated look or have the most expensive makeup when you're beginning. Your skill will grow as you practice. Practice makes perfect. Especially when it comes to makeup.
