Makeup is an art that anyone can enjoy. Many girls are taught that makeup is used to make you look more beautiful or that makeup is only for girls. But we can't make rules for makeup. Makeup comes in many different forms that sometimes, even guys can use. Girls should be taught that makeup doesn't make you prettier, but instead enhances your naturally beautiful features.

Makeup Facts

  1. Makeup has been around since the ancient egyptians.
    • The first cosmetics that the ancient egyptians made were made from copper and lead ore.
    • Egyptian women would paint their eyes by applying a dark color under the eyelid and they blackened their eyelashes.
    • Makeup was at first used to frighten enemies, show a social rank or make magic.
  2. Before blush, women used to pinch their cheeks to get them to look rosy.
  3. During the 1400s, a pale complexion was a popular trend. Many women would cause themselves to bleed many diferent waays. One way using leeches.

Makeup has been around for many, many years. It's a great way for anyone to express their creativity.
